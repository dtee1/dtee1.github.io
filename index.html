About

I am an engineer with a passion for designing, scaling, and maintaining software. I obtained an Electrical Engineering degree from Carleton University where my co-op experience was hardware focused. Upon completion of my degree, I immediately joined Pleora Technologies as a Test Automation Engineer then Product Verification Engineer where I was exposed to software, hardware, and system engineering. At Pleora, I had the opportunity to develop automated tests for an Artificial Intelligence web application. I enjoyed this software project and I decided to transition into software engineering. I am currently pursing a part time master's degree in Applied Artificial Intelligence at University of Ottawa. In my spare time, I develop software, contribute to open source projects, and invest in training and certification.
